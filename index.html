About Me

I’m a designer, developer, and problem-solver passionate about merging creativity with technology. As the founder of Aurion, I help businesses and individuals bring their digital ideas to life through web design, custom software, and AI-driven solutions. My work focuses on functionality, clean aesthetics, and user-centered experiences that make technology feel natural and empowering.

Every project I take on starts with understanding, the brand, the goals, and the people behind it. I believe design is more than visuals; it’s about creating seamless systems that tell a story, improve efficiency, and spark engagement. Whether I’m building a responsive website, developing automation tools, or refining a user interface, my goal is always to craft something purposeful and lasting.

Aurion was born from a desire to blend engineering precision with creative design. My background in Mechanical Engineering from the University of Pretoria taught me how to approach challenges analytically, while my passion for digital innovation taught me to think beyond boundaries. Together, these perspectives allow me to build solutions that are both technically sound and visually compelling.

Outside of client work, I’m constantly learning and experimenting with new technologies, from AI integration and UX design principles to digital storytelling. For me, growth is a continuous journey, and every new challenge is an opportunity to create something meaningful.
